Eveline

" Eveline" is a short story by James Joyce about a girl who dreams of leaving Ireland. Eveline becomes a surrogate mother to her siblings after their real mother's death. She works a menial, unsatisfying job.

Caline

Caline is sleeping in a field near her isolated home when she is awakened by a stopping train. This has never happened before. She has a brief meeting with some of the passengers. She wants to find out where they’re from and where they’re going.

The bet

At a dinner party a banker argues that capital punishment is preferable to life imprisonment. A young lawyer disagrees, saying that he would rather life in prison over death. They bet two million rubles that the lawyer can’t stay isolated for fifteen years.

Regret

A story written by Kate Chopin in 1897 , it tells the story of : An unmarried, fifty year-old country woman is called on to look after a neighbor’s children for a while. She gradually adjusts to their presence and needs.
